Admire of seeing people perfomance on the stage,
despite of unpleasant sound and tend to learn dancing as a hobby.

Since I was a kid, I'd like to show any of my skill to others. When the audience feel happy and laugh during my performance, I felt fully satisfy of all. As I become more maturity, I know that acting cute or just consider a comedy isn't easy that I think. So, I turned to find something which can replace it. Firstly I think that singing in front of people is very depends on the person voice. Actor also need to be trained a lot before so that you can act more similar to the character. At the end I choose dancing because it is quite simple and I can be another person during I dance.

Dancing is seperate to a lot of dance style, such as Popping, Locking, HipHop and still many. So, I joined NCCU dance club to learn any style of dance. There are different type of style that we can choose in every single day except weekend. During the class, they will inivite a professional teacher come towards to teach us in front of a mirror. And all the teachers are very kindly and humorous. I felt happy after I joined this club and I hopes I would not stop it because this is the only way that I can continue my hobby.
